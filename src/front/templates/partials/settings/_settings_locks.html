DRAFT versions use a single-editor lock: only one user may edit a given (domain, version) at a time; others land read-only until the holder closes the domain. There is no timeout — a lock left by a crashed browser is cleared here by force-unlock. Unlocking a version someone is actively editing will drop their edit rights on their next action.
